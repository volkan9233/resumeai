No subscriptions. ResumeAI is sold as a 1-year access license (one-time purchase).

We store the text you paste (resume/job description) only to provide the analysis and generate outputs. We may keep minimal technical logs (e.g., request time, error logs) for security, rate-limiting, and debugging.

We use cookies or similar identifiers only to remember unlock/access status after purchase and to operate the service.

1) Information you provide

  • Input text (resume and job description) you paste into the Service.
  • Contact details you provide during purchase or support requests (e.g., email).

2) Information collected automatically

  • Basic logs (e.g., request time, error logs) to keep the Service running and prevent abuse.
  • Cookies that may be required for sign-in/unlock state (e.g., to remember access after purchase).

3) How we use information

  • To provide the analysis and generate output.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Service (including rate-limiting and fraud prevention).
  • To provide customer support.

4) AI processing

The Service may send the text you submit to an AI provider to generate analysis and output. We do not sell your input text.

5) Payments

Payments are processed by our payment provider(s). We do not receive your full card details. The payment provider may collect and process information according to their own policies.

6) Data retention

We aim to minimize stored content. Unless otherwise stated, input text may be processed transiently to generate output. We may retain limited logs needed for security, fraud prevention, and debugging.

7) Your choices

  • You can avoid submitting personal data in your resume text if you prefer.
  • You may request deletion of support communications by emailing us (subject to legal/operational needs).

8) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
