Refund Policy (3 Days)

We offer a 3-day refund window from the time of purchase. If you're not satisfied, email support@resumeai.work within 3 days and include your purchase email and order/session ID. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

We may decline refund requests after the 3-day window. If you have technical issues, contact us first — we’ll help you resolve them as quickly as possible.

Eligibility

  • Technical issues: If the Service is unusable due to a verified technical problem on our side and we can’t resolve it within a reasonable time, you may be eligible for a refund.
  • Accidental duplicate purchase: If you purchased twice for the same account/email, we’ll refund the duplicate charge.

Not eligible

  • Change of mind after successful access/use.
  • Dissatisfaction with output style/content when the Service is functioning (output is automated and requires user review).
  • Failure to use the Service due to user device/browser restrictions.

How to request a refund

Email support@resumeai.work with: (1) the purchase email, (2) the approximate purchase date/time, and (3) a short description of the issue.

Processing

If approved, refunds are issued back to the original payment method. Timing depends on your bank/card provider.

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
