Refunds & Right to Cancel (14 Days)

If you are a consumer, you may have the right to cancel your purchase within 14 days without giving any reason. The cancellation period expires 14 days from the day after purchase.

How to request a refund / cancellation:
Paddle is our Merchant of Record and handles payments and refunds. The fastest way is to use the support/refund link in your purchase receipt email from Paddle.

If you can’t find the receipt, email us at support@resumeai.work with your purchase email and approximate purchase date/time, and we’ll help you locate the order and submit the request.

Note: Where a digital service is made available immediately after purchase, you may be asked at checkout to consent to immediate performance and acknowledge that you may lose your right to cancel once delivery/access has begun.

This policy is intended to align with Paddle’s consumer terms.
